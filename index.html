What is Merge Rot?

Merge Rot is the ultimate chaotic merge game where creativity and mayhem collide. Drop random items into a container and watch them merge into unexpected, hilarious, and disturbingly adorable creations[^5^]. Picture a surreal watermelon world where every move feels like a mad alchemist’s experiment. You toss bizarre items into a bowl, and through some strange magic, they fuse—morphing into increasingly odd, hilarious, and captivating masterpieces[^5^].

How to Play Merge Rot

Playing Merge Rot is refreshingly straightforward:

  1. Use the mouse to move your object above the cup.
  2. Click to drop the item.
  3. When two identical items collide, they merge into a larger, weirder version[^6^][^11^].
  4. Keep merging to discover all the wacky evolutions before the cup overflows!

There’s no complicated scoring system or timer—just pure merging fun and the joy of unlocking absurd combinations[^11^].
